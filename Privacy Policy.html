Privacy Policy

Smart Health Surveillance and Early Warning System

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, Age, and Location (entered by users).
  • Reported health symptoms.
  • Device and usage data (basic logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To detect possible water-borne diseases early.
  • To generate alerts for health officials when risks are high.
  • To improve the accuracy of our disease detection system.

3. Data Security

All personal data is stored securely with encryption. Only authorized health officials can access outbreak data, and individual reports are anonymized whenever possible.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only shared with government health departments and authorized agencies in case of disease outbreak alerts.

5. User Rights

  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
  • You have the right to know how your data is being used.
  • You can opt out of alerts by contacting our support team.
